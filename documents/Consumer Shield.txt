CONSUMER SHIELD - ENROLLMENT GUIDELINES

ACCOUNTS WE CAN ENROLL:

• Unsecured Credit Cards
• Business Credit/Loans given to individuals if the business is dissolved
• Unsecured lines of credit (Except Credit Unions)
• Apartment Back Rent (only if in collections)
• Medical Bills
• Cell Phone Bills
• USAA - as long as no other secured debt
• Deficiencies (Need Deficiency letter prior to enrollment)
• Auto Loan after Repo and sold
• Unsecured personal loans
• Utility bill
• Tribal loan - (Tribal doc signing required)
• Pay Day loans
• Peer to Peer loans - Like Pay day loans

ACCOUNTS WE CAN NOT ENROLL:

• Timeshares
• Mortgages
• Federal Student Loans
• Accounts with a judgement in place (Case by Case)
• IRS Tax Debt
• Cross Collateralized Accounts
• Furniture Stores that provide their own financing
• Loans from individuals
• Installment sales contracts
• Loans from credit unions - (All or Nothing)
• Direct loans from furniture stores
• Military Accounts
• AFES Accounts

BASIC GUIDELINES:

• Minimum Debt enrolled: $5,500 or more
• The payment minimum is $220
• Payment plan term: Minimum of 24 months Under $8,800 In Debt to a maximum of 36 months OVER $8,800
• The clients' initial payment must be within 18 days enrollment date
• Enrolled accounts must have a balance of No LESS than $100

GREEN STATES - GOOD TO GO:

AL, AK, AZ, AR, CA, CT, DE, DC, FL, GA, HI, ID, IL, IN, IA, KS, KY, LA, ME, MD, MA, MI, MN, MS, MO, MT, NE, NH, NM, NV, NY, NC, ND, OH, OK, PR, RI, SC, SD, TN, TX, UT, VA, VT, WA, WV, WI, WY

NON-SELL STATES - RED STATES:

CO, PA, NJ, OR

IMPORTANT NOTES:

• Consumer Shield does not provide specific creditor lists
• Eligibility is based on account TYPE, not specific creditor names
• Credit Union loans: All or Nothing rule applies
• USAA: Only if no other secured debt exists
• Furniture stores: NOT accepted if they provide their own financing
• Military accounts: NOT accepted (includes AFES accounts)
